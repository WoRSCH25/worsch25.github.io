WoRSCH 2025



Workshop on Random Simplicial

Complexes and Hypergraphs




1-2nd September, 2025 | Nottingham Trent University
Home Registration Schedule Directions

This two day workshop aims to bring together researchers working on random models in simplicial complexes, hypergraphs and other higher order structures. The workshop will provide an opportunity to share existing results and develop new models, of particular interest are new models for directed structures, and applications in complex networks and topological data analysis. By bringing together a range of researchers from different background we hope to provide a fertile environment for collaboration.
